## Customer Guide

### Creating an Account

1. **Navigate to the store:** Visit your tenant's store URL (e.g., `https://mystore.dukanest.com`)

2. **Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account":**
   - Located in the header or on the login page

3. **Fill in your information:**
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm Password

4. **Submit the form:**
   - You'll receive a verification email
   - Click the verification link in the email

5. **Log in:**
   - Use your email and password to access your account

### Browsing Products

1. **Homepage:**
   - View featured products
   - Browse by categories
   - See special offers and promotions

2. **Product Categories:**
   - Click on category names in the navigation
   - Filter products by category

3. **Product Search:**
   - Use the search bar in the header
   - Enter product name, SKU, or keywords
   - Results show matching products

4. **Product Filters:**
   - Filter by price range
   - Filter by availability (in stock)
   - Sort by price, name, or date

### Viewing Product Details

1. **Click on a product** to view details:
   - Product images (click to zoom)
   - Product description
   - Price and availability
   - Product variants (size, color, etc.)
   - Reviews and ratings

2. **Select variants** (if available):
   - Choose size, color, or other options
   - Price updates based on selection

3. **Add to Cart:**
   - Select quantity
   - Click "Add to Cart" button
   - Product is added to your cart

### Shopping Cart

1. **View Cart:**
   - Click the cart icon in the header
   - See all items in your cart
   - View cart total

2. **Update Quantities:**
   - Use +/- buttons or enter quantity directly
   - Cart updates automatically

3. **Remove Items:**
   - Click "Remove" next to any item
   - Item is removed from cart

4. **Continue Shopping:**
   - Click "Continue Shopping" to browse more products

5. **Proceed to Checkout:**
   - Click "Checkout" button
   - You'll be redirected to checkout page

### Checkout Process

1. **Shipping Address:**
   - Enter your shipping address
   - Or select a saved address
   - Add new address if needed

2. **Billing Address:**
   - Enter billing address
   - Or use same as shipping

3. **Review Order:**
   - Review all items
   - Check quantities and prices
   - Verify shipping address

4. **Payment:**
   - Select payment method
   - Enter payment details
   - Complete payment

5. **Order Confirmation:**
   - You'll receive an order confirmation email
   - Order number is displayed
   - You can track your order

### Support Tickets

#### Create Support Ticket
1. Go to "Support" page
2. Click "Create Ticket"
3. Fill in the form:
   - Subject
   - Department (Sales, Support, etc.)
   - Priority (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
   - Description
   - Attach files if needed
4. Submit ticket

#### View Tickets
1. Go to "Support" â†’ "My Tickets"
2. View all your tickets
3. **View Ticket Details:**
   - Click on a ticket
   - See conversation history
   - View status and priority

#### Reply to Ticket
1. Open a ticket
2. Type your message in the reply box
3. Attach files if needed
4. Click "Send Reply"

#### Ticket Status
- **Open:** New ticket, awaiting response
- **In Progress:** Being handled by support team
- **Resolved:** Issue resolved, awaiting confirmation
- **Closed:** Ticket closed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the email.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, go to "My Account" â†’ "Profile" and update your email address.

**Q: Do I need an account to shop?**
A: No, you can shop as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders and save addresses.

### Orders

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Go to "My Account" â†’ "Orders", click on your order, and view the tracking information.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Yes, if the order hasn't been shipped yet. Go to your order details and click "Cancel Order".

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Payment methods vary by store. Common methods include credit cards, PayPal, and mobile money (M-Pesa).

### Products

**Q: How do I know if a product is in stock?**
A: Product pages show stock availability. "In Stock" means available, "Out of Stock" means unavailable.

**Q: Can I request a product that's out of stock?**
A: Some stores allow backorders. Contact the store owner for more information.

**Q: Are product prices final?**
A: Prices are as displayed. Shipping costs are added during checkout.

### Shipping

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Shipping times vary by store and location. Check the store's shipping policy or contact them directly.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Shipping options vary by store. Check the store's shipping policy during checkout.

**Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?**
A: Contact the store owner immediately if the order hasn't shipped yet.

### Returns & Refunds

**Q: What is your return policy?**
A: Return policies vary by store. Check the store's return policy page or contact them directly.

**Q: How do I return a product?**
A: Contact the store owner through support tickets or email to initiate a return.

**Q: How long do refunds take?**
A: Refund processing times vary. Contact the store owner for specific information.

### Support

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Use the "Support" page to create a ticket, or contact the store owner directly via email.

**Q: How quickly will I get a response?**
A: Response times vary by store. Most stores respond within 24-48 hours.

## Tips & Best Practices

### Shopping Tips
1. **Create an account** for faster checkout and order tracking
2. **Save addresses** to speed up future purchases
3. **Use wishlist** to save products for later
4. **Check product reviews** before purchasing
5. **Read product descriptions** carefully

### Account Security
1. **Use a strong password** (minimum 8 characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
2. **Don't share your password** with anyone
3. **Log out** when using shared computers
4. **Keep your email updated** for order notifications

### Order Management
1. **Review your order** before completing checkout
2. **Save order confirmations** for your records
3. **Track your orders** regularly
4. **Contact support** if you have questions
